# Become an AI-Fluent Product Manager

### A free, 3-week curriculum for non-technical PMs who need to lead AI products with confidence

---

Most PMs managing AI products are faking it.

They nod along in engineering reviews. They use the right words — "RAG," "fine-tuning," "hallucination" — without knowing what they mean. They can't push back on a bad technical proposal because they don't know enough to spot one.

This curriculum fixes that.

In 3 weeks, you'll go from confused to confident: able to evaluate AI proposals, define success metrics, spot risks early, and lead informed conversations with engineers, executives, and customers.

**No coding required. No math. Just the mental models you actually need.**

---

## What You'll Be Able to Do

By the end of Week 3:

- Explain how LLMs work to a CEO in 5 minutes — accurately
- Evaluate a technical proposal: "Should we fine-tune, use RAG, or just prompt?"
- Write user stories and acceptance criteria for AI features
- Define success metrics that tie AI quality to business outcomes
- Spot hallucination, bias, cost spiral, and privacy risks before they become incidents
- Write a complete AI Feature PRD
- Have peer-level conversations with engineers about model tradeoffs

## The Curriculum

### [Week 1 — AI Foundations](AI_PM_Curriculum_Syllabus.md)
*~12–15 hours*

Build the mental models that underpin everything else: how LLMs actually work, why they hallucinate, what a context window is, and how to evaluate the commercial model landscape (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Llama).

**Units:** What Are LLMs · LLM Behavior & Limitations · Commercial Model Landscape

**Deliverable:** LLM Capability Matrix — compare 3 models on your use case

---

### [Week 2 — Retrieval, Agents & Architecture](AI_PM_Curriculum_Week2.md)
*~12–15 hours*

Learn how to augment LLMs with external data (RAG), build multi-step reasoning systems (Agents), and choose the right architecture for any use case. Includes the modern essentials: structured outputs, prompt caching, and reasoning models.

**Units:** RAG · Agents & Tool Use · Fine-Tuning vs. Prompting vs. RAG vs. Reasoning Models

**Deliverable:** Architecture Decision Tree — the right approach for your use case

---

### [Week 3 — Product Strategy, Metrics & Launch](AI_PM_Curriculum_Week3.md)
*~12–15 hours*

Bridge technical knowledge into product decisions. Assess AI feasibility, calculate ROI, define metrics, manage risks, and write the PRD that gets your AI feature built right.

**Units:** AI Product Strategy · Metrics, Evaluation & Risk Management

**Deliverable:** A complete 1-page AI Feature PRD
